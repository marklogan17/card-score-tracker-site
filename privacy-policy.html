Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 21, 2026

Overview

Card Game Score Tracker is designed with your privacy in mind. The app works entirely offline and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data Storage

All data — including player names, game definitions, and score history — is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. No data is sent to any server or third party.

Data Backup & Export

The app allows you to export and import your data as a backup file. These files are created and stored on your device. You control where backup files are saved and shared — the app does not upload them anywhere automatically.

Game Catalog

The app may fetch a catalog of game presets from a remote server. This request does not include any personal information or device identifiers.

Analytics & Tracking

This app does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies. No usage data is collected.

Permissions

The app may request access to device storage for importing and exporting backup files. No other permissions are required.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be included in an app update. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app's store listing page.
